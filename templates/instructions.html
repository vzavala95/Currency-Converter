Search Instructions

To use the search feature, type a topic into the search bar and click search.
Please limit your topic to one that is present on Wikipedia.
A new page will load with the search results, including the top image and the first several sentences from the Wikipedia page, and a relevant Youtube video.
The search bar is present on all pages of the search engine.

Random Article Instructions

To use the random article feature, click on the "Random Article" button present on the other pages.
This will take you to results page populated with a random article from Wikipedia.
This results page will have the same layout as the search results page.
There is a very slim chance that the random button will cause the site to crash.
This is a known issue, and is through no fault of the user.
If this occurs, please reload the page and the issue should resolve itself.

Featured Article Instructions

To use the featured article feature, click on the "Featured Article" button present on the other pages.
This will take you to a results page populated with the Wikipedia Featured Article for the day.
This results page will also have the same layout as the search results page.
